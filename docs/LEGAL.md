# OSINT Legal & Ethical Guidelines

## General Principles

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT) involves collecting and analyzing publicly available information. While the data sources used by this platform are public, operators must adhere to legal and ethical standards.

## Data Collection Guidelines

1. **Respect Terms of Service**: Each external API has terms of service. Ensure your usage complies with the provider's terms, especially regarding rate limits and acceptable use.

2. **Rate Limiting**: This platform implements rate limiting for all external APIs. Do not circumvent these limits.

3. **Data Minimization**: Collect only the data necessary for your investigation. Avoid bulk collection without a specific purpose.

4. **Data Retention**: Configure appropriate data retention policies. Delete data that is no longer needed.

5. **Access Control**: Use TLP classifications and role-based access control to restrict access to sensitive intelligence.

## Legal Considerations by Region

### United States
- Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA): Only access data that is publicly available
- Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA): Do not use this tool as a consumer reporting agency
- State privacy laws vary — check local regulations

### European Union
- GDPR applies to personal data of EU residents
- Ensure lawful basis for processing (legitimate interest, consent, etc.)
- Implement data subject access request (DSAR) procedures
- Honor right to erasure requests

### General
- Do not use this platform for harassment, stalking, or illegal surveillance
- Document your investigative purpose and legal authority
- Maintain audit logs for accountability

## Ethical Use

- Verify information before acting on it
- Consider the impact of your intelligence products on individuals
- Report vulnerabilities responsibly
- Share threat intelligence with the community when appropriate (respecting TLP levels)

## Disclaimer

This software is provided for legitimate intelligence analysis purposes. Users are responsible for ensuring their use complies with all applicable laws and regulations.
